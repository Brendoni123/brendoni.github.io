What I am working on

The game that I am currently working on is in development. This game will be a story based fighting game which will allow the player to explore a new world, fight new enemies, and make choices in the game which will impact the story. The game as of now has a lot to go, but you can see a preview of what I have made below. This game is also being coded using GameMaker Studio 2 and the pixel art for this game is made entirely by me using the Aesprite software.

If you are interested in this game or would like to inform me on what I could add to the game, please email me at 323bbreschard@frhsd.com or use the Email link under the Contact Me section of the footer.
